Bahasa Inggris adalah bahasa Jermanik yang pertama kali dituturkan di Inggris pada Abad Pertengahan Awal dan saat ini merupakan bahasa yang paling umum digunakan di seluruh dunia.[4] Bahasa Inggris dituturkan sebagai bahasa pertama oleh mayoritas penduduk di berbagai negara, termasuk Britania Raya, Irlandia, Amerika Serikat, Kanada, Australia, Selandia Baru, dan sejumlah negara-negara Karibia; serta menjadi bahasa resmi di hampir 60 negara berdaulat. RAMA : Hi, Shinta. How are you today?
SHINTA : Hi, Rama. I am fine. How about you?
RAMA : I am fine too. Today is the first day of our school holiday.
SHINTA : Yes, we will have 21 days on this school holiday.
RAMA: What will you do during this school holiday, Mary?
SHINTA : I will visit my grandfather and my grandmother at Yogyakarta.
RAMA : Oh, you will spend your holiday in Yogyakarta ?
SHINTA : Yes.
RAMA : How long you will stay there ?
SHINTA : My father said that we will stay Yogyakarta for about seven days.
RAMA : When you and your parents go to Yogyakarta.
SHINTA : On next monday. We will go there by train from Gambir Station.
RAMA : I think you will have visit to some interesting places too in Yogyakarta.
SHINTA : Yes, Rama. My mother asks my father to have a vacation during we stay in Yogyakarta. We will visit Sultan Palace, Prambanan Temple, Malioboro Street, and cave tubing at Pindul Cave in Gunungkidul. Maybe we will watch The Ramayana Ballet at Prambanan Theatre.
RAMA : Oh, my God. It is a really amazing leisure, Shinta. I wish I will visit those places sometime.
SHINTA : Thank you, Rama. By the way, how about you? What will you do during this school holiday, Rama?
RAMA : I am not sure but I think I will do some activities.
SHINTA : Like what, Rama?
RAMA : Maybe I will go fishing with my father, camping with my brother and my relatives. And the most one is help my parents cleaning our house.
SHINTA : Oh, that is great too, Rama. You are a really good boy when you would like to help your parents cleaning your house.
RAMA : Thank you, Shinta. I do that with pleasure.
SHINTA : Okay, Rama. I think I should go home right now. See you, Rama.
RAMA : Okay, Shinta. See you too.
