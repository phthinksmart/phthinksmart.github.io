17. (gpp)² × (gpp)⁶ ÷ (gpp)² × (gpp)¹⁰⁰ ÷ (gpp)¹⁰⁶​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(gpp)² × (gpp)⁶ ÷ (gpp)² × (gpp)¹⁰⁰ ÷ (gpp)¹⁰⁶

= (gpp)^(2 + 6 - 2 + 100 - 106)

= (gpp)^(8 - 2 + 100 - 106)

= (gpp)^(6 + 100 - 106)

= (gpp)^(106 - 106)

= (gpp)^0

= 1

[tex]\sf(gpp) {}^{2} \times \sf(gpp) {}^{6} \div \sf(gpp) {}^{2} \times \sf(gpp) {}^{100} \div \sf(gpp) {}^{106} [/tex]

[tex] = \sf(gpp) {}^{2 + 6 - 2 + 100 - 106} [/tex]

[tex] = \sf(gpp) {}^{8 - 2 + 100 - 106} [/tex]

[tex] = \sf(gpp) {}^{6 + 100 - 106} [/tex]

[tex] = \sf(gpp) {}^{106 - 106} [/tex]
