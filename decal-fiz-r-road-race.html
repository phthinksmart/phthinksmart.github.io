29. 17. Two runners, R and S, are in a 1500-metre race. The average speed of R is 6 m/s. Given that S runs 780 m in 2 minutes and maintains his speed for the rest of the race, find the difference in their finishing times in seconds.​


Jawaban:

Their finishing times gap = 3.85 seconds

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

x=1500 m

Vr=6 m/s

tr=x/Vr

tr=1500/6=250 seconds

Vs=780m/2 minutes

Vs=780/(2×60) seconds

Vs=780/120=6.5 m/seconds

ts=x/Vs

ts=1500/6.5=246.15 seconds

Gap = 250-246.15=3.85 seconds

So S are 3.85 seconds ahead
