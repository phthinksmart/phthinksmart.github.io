20. buatlah dialog berdasarkan dari 3 buah ini instruction dan permission beserta dengan artinya​


Jawaban:

Asking for Permission

1. Can I …?

Can I go with Mary tonight? (Bolehkah aku pergi dengan Mary malam ini)

Can I borrow your pen? (Bisakah kupinjam penamu?)

Can I try? (Bolehkah aku mencoba/mencicipi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “can” dalam permission sangat informal, bisa pula terkesan kurang sopan. Jadi, ada baiknya hanya digunakan dalam pembicaraan dengan orang-orang terdekat.

2. May I …?

May I have another cup of coffee? (Bolehkah saya minta secangkir kopi lagi)

May I see your daughter? (Bolehkah saya menemui anak perempuan anda?)

May I go to the bathroom? (Bolehkah saya pergi ke kamar mandi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “may” untuk saat ini terbilang lebih baku; baiknya digunakan dalam percakapan atau urusan formal.

3. Could I …?

Could I use the phone please? (Bisakah saya memakai teleponnya?)

Could I please hold the baby? (Bolehkah saya menimang bayinya?)

Could I eat this pizza? (Bolehkah saya makan pizza ini?)

4. Do you think I could …?

Do you think I could borrow the book? (Bolehkah saya meminjam bukunya?)

Do you think I could speak to my dad? (Bisakah saya berbicara dengan ayah saya?)

Do you think I could use your computer? (Bolehkah saya memakai komputermu?)

5. Would it be possible for me to …?

Would it be possible for me to study in this room? (Bolehkah saya belajar di ruangan ini?)

Would it be possible for me to meet Mr. Stephenson? (Bolehkah saya bertemu dengan Mr. Stephenson?)

Would it be possible for me to join your class? (Bolehkah saya ikut di kelas Anda?)

6. Would you mind if …?

Would you mind if I sit here? (Apakah Anda keberatan/bolehkah jika saya duduk di sini?)

Would you mind if I took a five minute break? (Apakah Anda keberatan jika saya beristirahat sebentar?)

Would you mind if I left now? (Apakah Anda keberatan jika saya pergi sekarang?)

atau bisa juga dengan menggunakan “Do you mind if I …?”

Do you mind if I sit here?

Do you mind if I leave now?

7. Is it okay if …?

Is it okay if I wait here? (Bolehkah saya menunggu di sini?)

Is it okay if we go now? (Bolehkah kami pergi sekarang?)

Is it okay if I wear this coat? (Bolehkah saya memakai mantel ini?)

27. Accomplished Activities


Part A

1. A: Have you done much exercises this week?

B: Yes, I have already been to aerobics class four times.

2. A: Have you played any sports this month?

B: No, I haven't had the time.

3. A: How many movies have you been to this month?

B: Actually, I haven't seen any yet.

4. A: Have you been to any interesting parties recently?

B: No, I haven't gone to any parties for quite a while.

5. A: Have you called any friends today?

B: Yes, I have already made three calls.

6. A: How many times have you gone out to eat this week?

B: I have eaten at fast food restaurants a couple of times.

Part B

1. A: Have you ever sung in a karaoke bar?

B: Yes, I had. I sang in one on my birthday.

2. A: Have you ever lost something valuable?

B: No, I had not. But my brother lost his camera on a trip once.

3. A: Have you ever got/gotten(*) a traffic ticket?

B: Yes, I had. Once I got a ticket and had to pay $50.

4. A: Have you ever seen a live concert?

B: Yes, I had. I saw U2 at the stadium last year.

5. A: Have you ever been late for an important appointment?

B: No, I had not. But my sister was 30 minutes late for her wedding.

NOTE: (*)

American English : get - got - gotten

British English : get - got - got

Pembahasan

Simple Past adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi di masa lampau dan telah selesai (tidak ada efeknya sampai saat dibicarakan/sekarang).

Formula kalimat:

(+) Subyek + verb 2

(-) Subyek + did + not + verb 1

(?) Did + subyek + verb 1

Contoh kalimat:

(+) She cooked noodles yesterday.

(-) She did not cook noodles yesterday.

(?) Did she cook noodles yesterday?

Kata keterangan waktu: yesterday, last week, two years ago, dan semua yang menyatakan telah lampau.

Present Perfect adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang dimulai dilakukan di masa lampau namun masih berlangsung hingga sekarang atau efeknya masih terasa hingga saat dibicarakan/sekarang.

Formula kalimat:

(+) Subyek + have/has + verb 3

(-) Subyek + have/has + not + verb 3

(?) Have/has + subyek + verb 3

Contoh kalimat:

(+) She has already called you.

(-) She has not already called you.

(?) Has she already called you?

Kata keterangan waktu: Biasanya hanya menggunakan "already", "just", atau "recently".

Pembahasan soal

Part A

1. A: Have you done much exercises this week? (Sudahkah kamu melakukan olah raga minggu ini?)

B: Yes, I have already been to aerobics class four times. (Ya, aku sudah ke kelas aerobik empat kali.)

2. A: Have you played any sports this month? (Sudahkah kamu melakukan suatu kegiatan olah raga bulan ini?)

B: No, I haven't had the time. (Tidak, aku tidak punya waktu.)

3. A: How many movies have you been to this month? (Berapa banyak film yang kamu nikmati bulan ini?)

B: Actually, I haven't seen any yet. (Sebenarnya, aku belum menonton sama sekali.)

4. A: Have you been to any interesting parties recently? (Sudahkan kamu pergi ke pesta-pesta yang menarik akhir-akhir ini?)

B: No, I haven't gone to any parties for quite a while. (Tidak, aku belum pernah pergi ke pesta manapun sudah sejak lama.)

5. A: Have you called any friends today? (Sudahkah kamu menelepon teman hari ini?)

B: Yes, I have already made three calls. (Ya, aku sudah menelepon tiga panggilan.)

6. A: How many times have you gone out to eat this week? (Berapa kali kamu pergi keluar untuk makan minggu ini?)

B: I have eaten at fast food restaurants a couple of times. (Aku sudah makan di rumah makan cepat saji beberapa kali.)

Part B

1. A: Have you ever sung in a karaoke bar? (Pernahkan kamu menyanyi di tempat karaoke?

B: Yes, I had. I sang in one on my birthday. (Ya, aku pernah. Aku menyanyi sekali di ulang tahunku.)

2. A: Have you ever lost something valuable? (Pernahkah kamu kehilangan sesuatu yang bernilai?)

B: No, I had not. But my brother lost his camera on a trip once. (Tidak, tidak pernah. Tetapi saudara laki-lakiku kehilangan kameranya sekali dalam perjalanan.)

3. A: Have you ever got/gotten a traffic ticket? (Pernahkah kamu mendapatkan karcis tilang?)

B: Yes, I had. Once I got a ticket and had to pay $50. (Ya, aku pernah. Begitu aku dapat dan aku harus membayar sebesar $50.)

4. A: Have you ever seen a live concert? (Pernahkah kamu menonton konser langsung?)

B: Yes, I had. I saw U2 at the stadium last year. (Ya, aku pernah. Aku menonton U2 di stadion tahun lalu.)

5. A: Have you ever been late for an important appointment? (Pernahkah kamu terlambat untuk datang ke perjanjian yang penting?)

B: No, I had not. But my sister was 30 minutes late for her wedding. (Tidak, aku tidak pernah. Tetapi saudara perempuanku terlambat 30 menit sewaktu hari pernikahannya.)
