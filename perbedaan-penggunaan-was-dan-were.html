1. apa perbedaan Was dan Were? dan bagaimana cara penggunaannya?​


Jawaban:

Was digunakan untuk kalimat yang subjeknya berupa first person singular (orang pertama singular) yaitu I dan third person singular (orang ketiga tunggal) yaitu she, he, dan it. Sedangkan, were digunakan untuk subjek second person singular dan plural (orang kedua tunggal dan jamak) yaitu you


2. apa perbedaan penggunaan was dan were dalam kalimat pasif


Jawaban:

was adlah to be nya I,she,he

were adalah to be nya you,they,we


3. perbedaan penggunaan was, were, are dalam kalimat?

To be Am,Are,Is digunakan dalam kalimat waktu sekarang.

Perbedaan penggunaan was, were, are dalam kalimat?

Jawaban:

* Was digunakan dalam kalimat PAST TENSE untuk SUBJECT tunggal: I, He, She It

* Were digunakan dalam kalimat PAST TENSE untuk SUBJECT jamak: You, We, They

* Are digunakan dalam PRESENT TENSE untuk SUBJECT jamak: You, We, They

Penjelasan:

1. Present Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menunjukkan suatu Kegiatan atau Peristiwa yg bersifat RUTINITAS (kebiasaan) dan FAKTA (kebenaran umum). Present Tense terbagi menjadi dua bentuk yaitu:

A. Present Tense Verbal Sentence

= kalimat yg ada KATA KERJA (verb)

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

(-) S + DO/DOES + S + VERB 1 + O

(?) DO/DOES + S + VERB 1 + O?

B. Present Tense Nominal Sentence

= Kalimat yg TIDAK ada KATA KERJA (verb)

(+) S + To Be (is/am/are) + O

(-) S + To Be (is/am/are) + not + O

(?) To Be (is/am/are) + S + O?

* I = am (tunggal orang pertama)

* is = he, she, it (tunggal)

* ARE = You, we, they (jamak)

2. Present Continues Tense adalah tenses yg menunjukkan Kegiatan yg Berlangsung sekarang

(+) S + ToBe (is/am/are) + VERB+ing + O

(-) S + ToBe (is/am/are) + not + VERB+ing + O

(?) ToBe (is/am/are) + S + VERB+ing + O?

* I = am (tunggal orang pertama)

* is = he, she, it (tunggal)

* ARE = You, we, they (jamak)

3. Past Tense adalah Tenses yg menunjukkan Kegiatan di waktu LAMPAU. Past Tense terbagi menjadi dua bentuk yaitu:

A. Past Tense Verbal Sentence

= Kalimat yg ADA KATA KERJA (verb)

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

(-) S + DID NOT + VERB 1 + O

(?) DID + S + VERB 1 + O?

B. Past Tense Nominal Sentence

= Kalimat yg TIDAK ADA KATA KERJA (verb)

(+) S + ToBe (was/were) + O

(-) S + ToBe (was/were) + not + O

(?) ToBe (was/were) + S + O?

* was = I, he, she, it (tunggal)

* were = You, we, they (jamak)

4. Past Continues Tense adalah tenses yg menunjukkan Kegiatan yg BERLANGSUNG di MASA LAMPAU

(+) S + ToBe (was/were) + VERB+ing + O

(-) S + ToBe (was/were) + Not + VERB+ing + O

(?) ToBe (was/were) + S + VERB+ing + O?

* was = I, he, she, it (tunggal)

* were = You, we, they (jamak)

4. Penggunaan dan perbedaan did / was / were dalam kalimat tanya


Jawaban:

did/was/were merupakan to be untuk tenses bentuk lampau.

did : simple pas tense

was/ were : past continuous tense

Penjelasan:

did digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kejadian yang terjadi pada masa lampau, misalnya : Did you watch tv last night?

was/were digunakan untuk menjelaskan kejadian di masa lampau dengan waktu yang lebih spesifik misalnya at 7 pm, digunakan juga untuk kejadian di masa lampau ketika ada kejadian lain yang terjadi tiba-tiba

Misalnya: was he sit there yesterday?

were they play outside when it was raining?


5. jelaskan perbedaan penggunaan did dan was were untuk past simple. sama contoh kalimatnya ya kalau bisa


Soal :

Perbedaan penggunaan did, was, dan were dalam simple past? Beserta contoh!


Jawab :

-> Did digunakan dalam simple past, bentuk verbal. Biasanya digunakan dalam rumus negatif dan introgative.

Pattern :

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did + not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?


Contoh : Rika makan nasi

(+) Rika ate rice.

(-) Rika did not eat rice.

(?) Did Rika eat rice?


-> was dan were digunakan dalam simple past, bentuk nominal. Yaitu kalimat yang tidak menunjukan pekerjaan. Was digunakan jika subjek berupa I, she, he, it (singular) dan were digunakan jika subjek berupa they, you, we, it (plural).

Pattern :

(+) S + was/were + A/N/A

(-) S + was/were + not + A/N/A

(?) Was/Were + S + A/N/A?


Contoh :

=> Dia (pr) sakit kemarin

(+) She was sick yesterday.

(-) She was not sick yesterday.

(?) Was she sick yesterday?


=> Mereka dirumahku kemarin malam

(+) They were in my home last night.

(-) They were not in my home last night.

(?) Were they in my home last night?


Note :

-> A/N/A = Adjective/Noun/Adverb

-> Adjective = kata sifat.

-> Noun = kata benda

-> Adverb = kata keterangan.

MENENTUKAN KALIMAT

1. Gerunds atau bukan:

a. Playing a kite was my hobby when I was a kid. (Gerund: playing)

b. We were diving in Bali to see some undersea creatures. (Bukan Gerund)

C. Bob moved to London that was surprising news. (Bukan Gerund)

d. Taking care of my family is my priority. (Gerund: taking care)

e. I am excited about cooking pastry lately. (Gerund: cooking)

2. Kalimat dengan menggunakan Verbs dan Gerunds:

a. I appreciate hearing your thoughts on this matter.

b. I am considering going on a trip to Japan next year.

c. Let's discuss opening a new branch in the city center.

d. I always enjoy visiting my grandparents during the holidays.

e. Would you mind helping me carry this heavy box upstairs?

3. Kalimat dengan menggunakan Verbs yang dapat diikuti oleh Gerunds atau Infinitives:

a. I started working on my project early in the morning.

b. I like hiking in the mountains on weekends.

c. He hates smoking because it's bad for his health.

d. She loves teaching children how to read and write.

4. Perbedaan penggunaan Gerunds dan Infinitives dalam kalimat:

a. Helen always remembers to return the book on time. (Infinitive: to return)

b. My mother remembered living in her hometown when she ate the traditional cakes. (Gerund: living)

Perbedaannya terletak pada arti kalimat, "to return" dalam kalimat a. menunjukkan aksi yang akan dilakukan, sedangkan "living" dalam kalimat b. menunjukkan kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa lalu.

c. I regret to tell you that your parents couldn't come here. (Infinitive: to tell)

d. I regret lending her some money. She never paid me back. (Gerund: lending)

Perbedaannya terletak pada arti kalimat, "to tell" dalam kalimat c. menunjukkan tindakan yang akan dilakukan, sedangkan "lending" dalam kalimat d. menunjukkan tindakan yang telah dilakukan di masa lalu.

e. I'm trying to read this novel for you. (Infinitive: to read)
