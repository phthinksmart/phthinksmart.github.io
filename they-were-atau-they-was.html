10. were - Where-?-bom-.- youShe-.-born-in - Blitar - wasbom-were- They-in-.- Malan​


Jawaban:Where were you born?She was born in BlitarThey were born in Malang

Pembahasan:

1. Were - Where-?-born-.- you

=> Where were you born?

(Dimana kamu lahir?)

Structure Kalimatnya:WH question => WhereTobe Past Tense => wereSubject Plural => youAdjective => born

2. She-.-born-in - Blitar - was

=> She was born in Blitar

(Di lahir di blitar)

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject singular => SheTobe Past => WasAdjective => bornComplement => in Blitar

3. born-were- They-in-.- Malang

=> They were born in Malang

(Mereka lahir di Malang)

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject Plural => TheyTobe Past => wereAdjective => BornComplement => in Malang

Rumus Nominal Sentence:

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + Adjective

(-) S + Tobe (was/were) + not + Adjective

(?) Tobe (was/were) + S + Adjective?

25. Alasan was untuk subjek I dan were untuk they..?? Dst


alasannya karena ketntuan untuk
kata subyek
I
You
They
We
He she dan It
dalam kalimat present continous tensr,
dan untuk kata sifat (adjective) dan kata keterangan (adverb)
biasanya di ikuti kata to be yaitu is, am, are
is, an pasts tensenya. adalah was
are past tense nya adalah were
maka I am. - I was contoh
I am studying, I am here, I am right
I was studying, I was here, I was right
you, they, we + are/ were
He/she/ It, is / was
he is here now, he is studying at the moment
He was here yesterday, he was studying
was dan were adalah( kata lampau) untuk menunjang subyek dalam kalimat tertentu
