3. bahasa inggrisnya anakku sayang


The English translated phrase of 'anakku sayang' can be various depending on the preference as, again, it has term of endearment in it. That is 'sayang'. Thus, in English, the common translation of it will be 'my beloved child'.

Pembahasan:

In doing translation, several stuff has to be considered in mind such as the context and appropriate vocabulary. The phrase 'anakku sayang' can be said by parents mostly or by priests/ vicars. The terms of endearment used can be various, not only 'beloved'. Here are some of the other proper translation.

My dear childMy dearest childMy loving child/ son/ daughterMy endearing child/boy/girl

The word-by-word translation is also not recommended as it makes odd translated phrase instead of easily understood one, especially for the native speaker. For example, if the phrase 'anakku sayang'  is translated literally word by word, it will be 'my child love'.
