16. Cara membuat jus wortel dalam bahasa inggris dan dengan artinya


Jawaban:

ingredients:

2 carrots

3 tomatoes

2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Sweet white condensed milk (to taste

Lemon water (to taste, if desired)

Shaved ice (to taste)

How to make

1.Cut carrots and tomatoes into small pieces.

2.Blend carrots and tomatoes until smooth

3.add sugar, sweetened condensed milk and shaved ice then blend again.

4. Add a little water if the juice feels too thick.

5.Filter tomato carrot juice into a serving glass.

5.Drink while still cold.

25. Cara membuat jus wortel dalam bahasa inggris dan dengan takarannya


How to make carrot juice :
Ingredients : 3 carrots, ice cube, a bag of nut milk, a glass of water.
How to make :
1. Wash the carrots.
2. Chop the carrots until small pieces.
3. Add them to the blender.
4. Add one glass of water.
5. Blend on medium until the ingredients are well pulverized.
6. Place a nut milk bag into 1 1/2 of small cups.
7. Then, pour in the blended ingredients.
8. Squeeze as much liquid out of the nut milk bag as possible
9. Your carrot juice is ready to serve !
