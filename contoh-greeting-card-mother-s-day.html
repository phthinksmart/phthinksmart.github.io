13. create a greeting card based on the situations below a.your aunt has a new baby. b.your friends lost her mother. c.to celebrate mother day,you write a greeting card for your beloved mother


Membuat greeting card atau kartu ucapan berdasarkan situasi-situasi berikut adalah melihat dari konteks perayaannya terlebih dahulu, sehingga kita dapat menentukan kalimat ucapan apa yang seharusnya dikatakan. Biasanya kita memberikan ucapan selamat atau mengirimkan greeting card pada momen-momen seperti perayaan ulang tahun, wisuda, pernikahan, dan lain-lain. Berikut ini adalah pembahasannya lebih lanjut.

Pembahasan

1. Your aunt has a new baby

Dear aunt,

We are overjoyed at the arrival of the new member! Congratulations! Wishing you and uncle lots of happiness with your little one.


2. Your friend lost her mother

Please accept my sincere condolences on your loss. May God be with you in this hour of grief.


3. To celebrate mother’s day, you write a greeting card for your beloved mother

Dear mom,

I admire you, I love you, but the most of all, I feel blessed to be your child. I really appreciate all that you have done for me over the years. Happy mother’s day mama!
