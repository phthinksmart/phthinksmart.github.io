How To Make Boiled Noodle Soup

Tool

- teflon

- spoon and fork

- bowl

Material

- 1 pack of onion-flavored instant noodles

- 2 eggs

- 4 pieces of cayenne pepper (according to taste)

- 1 clove of garlic

- 2 cloves of red onion

- 2 pieces of cabbage

- water to taste

Steps

- Boil water until it boils. Put the whole scrambled eggs into the water. If it is cooked or feels solid, remove and drain. Replace the noodles.

- Chop the garlic, shallots, cabbage and cayenne pepper. - Put it in the boiled water, stir well.

- Add all the noodle seasonings, stir. If the noodles are almost cooked, beat one egg, pour it into the broth. Stir well until the eggs are cooked and the gravy thickens.

- Pour the noodles into a bowl and serve with boiled eggs. Special gravy boiled noodles are ready to be enjoyed.

How to make a boiled noodle Ingredients :

one Indomie onion flavour
3 bird eye chilly, cut in small ( I like spicy, you can reduced or skip )
one egg
3 – 5 leaves of Bok Coy, Chaisim or green mustard leaves, cut in 3cm ( or any leaves you like to try )
400 ml water
How to cook :

pour 400 ml water in a pan, add the chilly also all the ingredients from the Indomie ( seasoning and oil ), boil it in medium heat (if you wanna get the spicy from the chilly, boil it at lease 10 minutes) until fragrant.

add the leaves and noodles in the boiled water until soft , and the noodles is ready to put in the bowl.
