14. Tolong terjemahkan Ke Bahasa Indonesia Rafflesia has brownish, scale like leaves and fleshly, smelling flowers of various sizes, from few inches to meter big in diameter. We usually notice its large fleshly flowers. There is a deep well in the centre of the flowers. The sexual organs are located beneath the rim of the disk. Male and female flowers are separate. The reddish brown colors of the petals, sprinkled with white freckles produce unpleasant stench, similar to rotting flesh. This would attract flies and insects which help disperse the seeds. Rafflesia is parasite, which means it takes the nutrient out of its host.
