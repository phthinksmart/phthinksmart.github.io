25. Tolong buat percakapan 5 orang tentang menawarkan bantuan dalam bahasa inggris


Untuk membuat sebuah percakapan mengenai menawarkan bantuan, kita harus mengetahui ekspresi apa saja yang digunakan terlebih dahulu. Berikut ini adalah contoh percakapan 5 orang mengenai menawarkan bantuan dalam bahasa Inggris.

A : B, you look so confused. What happened?

B : I lost my science book. I didn't find it in my bag.

C : Did you look at your drawer?

B : Yes, I did. But it wasn't in there.

D : Can I help you to find it?

E : Yes, let us help you to find your book.

A : Where did you last put your book?

B : I put it on my desk. And then, the bell was ringing and I went to the canteen.

E : Did your book have your tag name?

B : Yes, I put my name on my book.

C : Don't worry, B. We will help you to find it.

B : Thank you, guys.

Pembahasan

Menawarkan bantuan/layanan (offering help/service) adalah salah satu ungkapan yang paling umum digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris. Kita dapat menggunakan ini ketika kita ingin menawarkan bantuan kita kepada orang lain.

Beberapa pola menawarakan bantuan/layanan (pаtterns of offering help/service) аre

1. Mаy + I + verb 1?

For exаmple:

Mаy I help you?Mаy I give you а hаnd?

2. Would you like me to + verb 1?

For exаmple:

Would you like me to cook our dinner?Would you like me to bring your books to the clаss?

3. Cаn + I + verb 1?

For exаmple:

Cаn I help you?Cаn I bring this to your tаble?

4. Do you wаnt me + to + verb 1?

For exаmple:

Do you wаnt me to write this letter for you?Do you wаnt me to iron your clothes?Pelajari Lebih LanjutMateri tentang asking for help dan offering for help pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/2927693Materi tentang the patterns of offering help /service pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/16636781Materi tentang dialog asking giving accepting and refusing help pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/2368353Detail Jawaban
