1. contoh Recount text tentang pengalaman di sekolah smp


The Amazing School Trip To Bali

When I was in Junior High School, my classmates and I went to Bali for a school trip. We had to take a bus from Madiun to Bali. It took us almost the whole day to get to Bali. However, the bus trip was so much fun because my classmates were very entertaining.

After we arrived in Bali, we went straight to the Monkey Temple. In that place, we could see a lot of cheeky yet cute monkeys. One monkey managed to steal my friend's water bottle. It was hilarious! I couldn't stop laughing at him.

Next, we went to Nusa Dua Beach. My friends and I decided to ride "banana boat". After trying to bargain for half hour with the vendor, we finally managed to get a decent price. The banana boat turned out to be the coolest thing we did in Bali. We rode a boat that looked like a banana, and it was pulled by a boat. The boat kept on making a U turn quickly until we are all fell off the boat. It was such a great experience!

Finally, it was time to end the trip. We had to say goodbye to Bali. I felt so sad but I couldn't really do anything. However, I knew I will go back to Bali again one day. With that thought in mind, I finally was able to get on the bus and went back to Madiun.

Hope this helps!

4. contoh recount text pengalaman pramuka


Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

Menggunakan Past tenseMenggunakan action verbmenggunakan thinking verb.
Pembahasan

Recount Text tentang pengalaman kegiatan pramuka:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

17. contoh recount text tentang pengalaman sedih


The Ocean of Sorrow

 

In the afternoon, a beautiful girl with a red hooded named Aisha looked sad on the seashore. She was sitting in the dock and kept looking at the blue ocean. The waves came crashing her feet but did not make her moved to another place. The shore of wind which blew till stabbed her bone did not make her eyes diverted to ocean.

That day, the sky was cloudless. She kept looking at the blue sea till the horizon began to return. She saw the fishermen who were getting back from the sea and bringing fishes quite a lot. The fishermen were greeted by their family who had been waiting for them at the edge of the ocean. Seeing something very emotional there, her tears soaked her cheeks. She thought of his family who had gone and left her alone.

That girl hated the sea because that’s where her family lost and died. It happened about 4 months ago when the ship that brought them from across the island was on fire and sank in the ocean. All her family died and lost including her mother, father, and brother. However, Aisha survived from the accident.

Until now, Aisha always comes to the beach every day to look at the ocean because from that place, she could remember her beloved family and remember the happy memories that they went through together. Her tears were rolling down her cheeks until the sun disappeared entirely behind the ocean.


18. Contoh recount text tentang pengalaman orang lain


My friend's Experience to Ride Motorcycle
One day, when I was ten years old, my father bought an old motorcycle. That was ” Honda 75″. I think it was small light object and easy to ride it. I persuaded my father to teach me to ride ” Honda 75 “. Firstly, my father refused my request and promised that he would teach me two or three years later, but I still whimpered. Finally, my father surrendered and promised to teach me.
He began to teach me riding the motorcycle around a field in my village. My father was very patient to give me some directions. I was very happy when I realized my ability to ride a motorcycle. ” Yes, I can “.
One day later, when I was alone at home, I intended to try my riding ability. So, myself tried bravely. All ran fluently in the beginning, but when I was going back to my home and I must passed through a narrow slippery street, I got nervous. I lost my control and I fell to the ditch.
After that, I told my father about the last accident. I imagined my father would be angry and never let me ride again. But the reality is exactly on the contrary, my father was very proud of me. He just gave me some advices and since that accident, I got my father’s permission to ride motorcycle.

24. Contoh recount text pengalaman di pesantren


I was in pesantren called Kun Anta. In that pesantren the girls and boys were in different room. Sometimes, me and my friends teased the girls, so we had fun everyday. There were so many activities, like read Qur'an, prayed on time, and prayed before do something too. In the end, we must knew everything about Islam. I felt happy to known about Nabi Muhammad saw. His story was very interested. Soon I will visit the pesantren again and remembered when i was here. I was very happy and enjoyed in that pesantren.

Translate:

Aku telah berada di pesantren yang bernama Kun Anta. Di pesantren itu perempuan dan laki laki berada di kamar yang berbeda. Sesekali, aku dan teman-temanku menjahili para perempuan, jadi kita dapat kesenangan setiap hari. Disini banya sekali aktivitas, seperti membaca Qur'an, sholat tepat waktu, dan berdia sebelum melakukan sesuatu. Pada akhirnya, kita harus tau apa saja tentang Islam. Aku merasa senang untuk mengetahui ceruta nabi Muhammad saw. Ceritanya sangat menarik. Suatu saat aku akan berkunjung lagi dan mengingat ketika aku berada disini. Aku sangat merasa bahagia dan menikmati saat di pesantren itu.

Penjelasan:

Recount : menceritakan kembali

Dalam recount pasti ada verb 3 yang menunjukkan past(masa lalu). Saya nggak yakin sama karangan yg saya buat, inggris saya ga terlalu bagus

30. Contoh Recount Text Tentang Pengalaman Di Pantai?


A Beautiful Day on Parangtritis Beach
Last holiday, my family and I went to Parangtritis beach in Yogyakarta. We had arrived in Yogyakarta several days before. We had gone around the city and at the last day we finally came to the beach and we rent a cottage near it. We arrived at 10 o’clock in the morning. It was a perfect time to enjoy the beach. The scenery was so wonderful and I felt that the sun kissed me though its bright light. I felt blessed because I could enjoy one of God’s masterpieces. My parents sat together under their umbrella. My younger brothers played sand. My older sister took her self picture and I enjoyed the wind blowing.
My mother had prepared a bucket of foods and some drinks for us. She had arranged to have a picnic time at the beach. She brought many bread toast with beef and cheese on it, some fruit salads, and orange juice. Those were so fresh and perfect companion for the perfect moment we had. After having picnic, my father challenged us to fly with paragliding. That was an amazing offer for me. But, my sister seemed afraid of it. Then, we accepted the challenge. I was so excited, and so were my brothers. My mother and my sister just watched us.After that, my mother and sister took their turn, but it was not paragliding. They were interested to ride a horse. While they rode the horses and my father kept his eyes on them, my brothers and I were walking around and looking for some beautiful snails. I got some and my brothers got starfishes. They looked cute. Therefore, we let them go then and didn’t take it. It was almost 4 o’clock in the afternoon and we went to the cottage. I felt so happy to have great experience at Parangtritis beach. I wished someday I could come back
