75% dan 0%

Parental = HHRr >< HhR

Filial = HHRR, HHRr, HhRR, HhRr, HHRr, HHrr, HhRr, Hhrr

Persentase genotip:

Kelinci hitam berbulu halus = HHRR, HHRr, HhRR, HhRr, HHRr, HhRr

Kelinci hitam berbulu halus = 6/8.100%  = 75%

Kelinci putih berbulu halus = 0%.
