9. Luas permukaan Bola

Lp bola = 4 × π × r × r

[tex]lp = 4 \times \pi \times r \times r[/tex]

[tex]lp = 4 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 7 \: cm \times 7 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]lp = \frac{4 \times 22 \times 7 \times 7}{7} [/tex]

[tex]lp = \frac{4.312 \: cm}{7} [/tex]

[tex]lp \: bola = 616 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

10. Volume Bola

Volume Bola= ⁴/3 × π × r × r × r

[tex]v \: = \frac{4}{3} \times \pi \times r \times r \times r[/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{4}{3} \times \frac{22}{7} \times 7 \: cm \times 7 \: cm \times 7cm[/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{4}{3} \times \frac{22 \times 7 \times 7 \times 7}{7} [/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{4}{3} \times \frac{7.546}{7} [/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{4}{3} \times 1.078 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{1.078 \: cm \times 4}{3} [/tex]

[tex]v = \frac{4.312 \: cm}{3} [/tex]

[tex]v = 1.437.33 \: {cm}^{3} [/tex]

27. rumus bola pada bangun ruang


Rumus Luas Bola

L = 4 x luas lingkaran

   = 4 x π r²

   = 4 π r²

Rumus Volume Bola

V = 4 x volume kerucut

    = 4 x 1/3 π r² t

V = 4 x 1/3 π r² r
    = 4 x 1/3 π r³
    = 4/3 π r³
